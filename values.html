Our Values

At Upstream, we think how we work is as important as what we do to help our clients’ succeed. Here’s what guides us:

Perspective and experience create value

We’ve been there before. With a steady hand, we steer clients to the right course.

Responsibility and accountability matter

We set out to deliver what we promise – no surprises, no excuses.

Seek breakthroughs

We thrive on the challenge of delivering new solutions to old problems.

The team has to work

We quickly build custom teams that work together to deliver just what clients need –and not what they don’t.

Our reputation is your reputation

Our goal is that every client can look back and say, “we made the right choice when we hired them.”

Have some fun along the way

We work hard. But we also know that there’s more to life, and we believe that perspective ensures that we focus on what matters most to our clients.
