Our Values

At Upstream, we think how we work is as important as what we do to help our clients’ succeed. Here’s what guides us:

Perspective and experience create value.

We’ve been there before and, with a steady hand, we steer clients to the right course.

Responsibility and accountability matter.

We set out to deliver what we promise – no surprises, no excuses.

Seek breakthroughs.

We thrive on the challenge of delivering new solutions to old problems.

The team has to work.

We build custom teams to deliver just what clients need – and not what they don’t – and then we work as a team.

Our reputation is your reputation.

We hope that every client can look back and say, “we made the right choice when we hired them.”

Have some fun along the way.

We work hard but we also know that there’s more to life, and we believe that perspective ensures we focus on what matters.
