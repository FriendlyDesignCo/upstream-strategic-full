Experience

Experience matters, especially when the stakes are high. With more than 20 years of experience running successful communications campaigns, the team at Upstream Strategic Communications knows how to deliver results that make a difference.

  • We have represented a diverse range of clients, from companies, law firms and trade associations to coalitions and non-profit organizations. The focus of those engagements has been equally varied.
  • In the financial services arena, we have worked with several mortgage lenders and insurers.
  • Experience in the healthcare sector has included multiple clients in the mental health and addiction treatment field, several disease groups as well as companies in the life sciences and diagnostics industries.
  • Education experience has included non-profit organizations and trade associations representing higher education accrediting agencies as well elementary and secondary education issues.
  • In the field of energy and sustainability, we have represented utilities working through regulatory changes as well as several advocacy organizations addressing sustainability and conservation policies.
  • And among defense contractors, we have worked with a diverse range of companies facing intense public and governmental scrutiny.
